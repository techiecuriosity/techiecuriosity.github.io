Frequently Asked Questions

What is a dosha?

In Ayurvedic medicine, doshas are the three energies that define every person's makeup. These are Vata (air and space), Pitta (fire and water), and Kapha (earth and water).

How can I find my dosha type?

Take our free dosha quiz to discover your unique constitution. The quiz evaluates your physical characteristics, mental traits, and lifestyle habits to determine your dominant dosha.

Is this dosha quiz accurate?

Yes, our dosha quiz is based on traditional Ayurvedic principles and modern research. It evaluates multiple aspects of your physical, mental, and emotional characteristics for accurate results.
