Discover Your Ayurvedic Dosha

Take our free quiz to understand your unique mind-body type

Ayurveda, the ancient Indian system of medicine, recognizes three fundamental mind-body types known as doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. Each person has a unique combination of these doshas that influences their physical, mental, and emotional characteristics.

Understanding your dosha can help you make better lifestyle choices, improve your health, and achieve greater balance in your life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a dosha?

A dosha is a fundamental mind-body type in Ayurveda. There are three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. Each person has a unique combination of these doshas that influences their physical, mental, and emotional characteristics.

How accurate is this quiz?

This quiz is designed to give you a general understanding of your dosha constitution. For a more detailed analysis, we recommend consulting with an Ayurvedic practitioner.

Can my dosha change?

While your basic dosha constitution remains the same throughout your life, the balance of your doshas can change due to factors like diet, lifestyle, stress, and environment.
