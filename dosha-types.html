Dosha Types

In Ayurveda, there are three primary dosha types that make up our unique constitution. Each person has a unique combination of these doshas, with one or two usually being dominant.

Vata Dosha

Elements: Air + Space

Physical Characteristics:

  • Light, thin build
  • Dry skin and hair
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Irregular digestion

Mental Characteristics:

  • Creative and enthusiastic
  • Quick to learn and quick to forget
  • Lively and energetic
  • Prone to worry and anxiety

Pitta Dosha

Elements: Fire + Water

Physical Characteristics:

  • Medium build
  • Warm body temperature
  • Strong digestion
  • Prone to inflammation

Mental Characteristics:

  • Intelligent and focused
  • Competitive and goal-oriented
  • Strong leadership qualities
  • Prone to anger and irritability

Kapha Dosha

Elements: Earth + Water

Physical Characteristics:

  • Strong, sturdy build
  • Thick, oily skin
  • Slow digestion
  • Prone to weight gain

Mental Characteristics:

  • Calm and steady
  • Loyal and patient
  • Good memory
  • Prone to attachment and resistance to change

Understanding Your Dosha Balance

Most people have a combination of two doshas, with one being more dominant. Your dosha balance can change throughout your life due to factors like:

  • Age
  • Season
  • Diet
  • Lifestyle
  • Environment
